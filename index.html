About Me

Hello! I'm Oxa, a passionate 3D artist specializing in character design and digital fashion. With 5 years of experience in the field, I create immersive 3D experiences that blend art and technology.

My work focuses on bringing imaginative characters to life through detailed modeling and animation. I'm proficient in tools like Blender, Marvelous Designer, Three.js, and Unity.

When I'm not modeling or coding, you can find me exploring new digital fashion trends or creating concept art. I'm always exploring new techniques at the intersection of art and technology.
